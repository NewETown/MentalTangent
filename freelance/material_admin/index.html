Welcome to Buddy.

The Buddy Dashboard is your portal to using the BuddyAPI, an easy-to-use service that is accessible from any connected device. The BuddyAPI delivers device data to your business quickly and easily. No complicated agents, engineering dependencies, specialized hardware or firmware.

Why Buddy?

Building your own data solution is an extremely complicated, expensive, and time-consuming process. At Buddy, we focus on empowering our clients to build the best connected devices on the market by removing the hassle of building and managing a full-service back-end.

Your First Steps.

Getting started with Buddy is as easy as one, two, three.

One.

Create an account using the "GET STARTED" button below or the "REGISTER" button at the top of the page.

Two.

Review our quickstart guide for your platform.

Three.

Build and run your app. Your data is instantly visible in your Buddy Dashboard.

Now what?

The digitial world is your oyster. Your connected device or application is hooked-up to an extremely powerful back-end service that allows you to breeze through your development cycle. Send yourself telemetry, register users, make something awesome using any combination of our powerful APIs. After you publish your app or start selling your device, contact us for dashboards to hang in your office or use as presentation tools for your clients.
